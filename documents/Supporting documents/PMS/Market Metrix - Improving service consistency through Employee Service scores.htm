Improving service consistency through Employee Service scores

Client: Luxury Hotel

Solution: Add management control to the customer experience by capturing Employee Service scores.

 

One of the country’s top luxury lodging companies, this client boasts some of the industry's most innovative and successful travel programs and has embraced Customer Metrix™ as a core of its daily operations and management decision making. 

This organization is committed to superior customer service and considers guest satisfaction as its #1 corporate goal.  This critical measure, obtained thorough the guest feedback system provided by Market Metrix, is discussed daily at each hotel and plays an important role in employee compensation and the performance review process. 

This company knows that delivering the right service level can improve customer loyalty and have financial benefit.  Therefore, in addition to hotel and company-wide reports, individual room attendants, food servers, and other employees receive individual scores based on guest evaluations.  Using these employee rankings, management can anticipate and control the levels of service that each department and each hotel will provide.  This is especially critical for this company because competition among luxury hotels is based primarily on service.

“We had a client satisfaction process in place for many years; however, we wanted to take the process to the next level. We recognized that we needed to engage our clients in the feedback process on a continual basis and begin measuring loyalty to improve client retention and improve long-term growth,”

“Market Metrix has the intellectual horsepower to deliver more than just data.  Market Metrix has the smarts, experience, analysis, user interface, credibility, and comparative data to help us achieve our goals.”
