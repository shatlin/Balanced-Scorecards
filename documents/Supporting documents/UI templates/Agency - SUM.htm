We're SUM Agency

We are a marketing agency founded when Area Creative joined forces with NOS to increase our ability to deliver better strategic and creative results to our clients locally, nationally and internationally.

History of Area Creative

Area Creative was founded in 2004. In its two-and-a-half years in business Area won several ADDY Awards for efforts in marketing, design, promotion, and web development.


Area Creative.com website back in 2004

Clients Area worked with (and who we will continue to work with under the SUM brand) included National Roofing, DW Turner, New Mexico Finance Authority, UNMH, PrimeSource Mortgage, Dion’s Pizza, Frank Frost Photography, and the Albuquerque Community Foundation.

History of NOS

NOS was founded in December 2003 by Juan Manuel Teixidó and Martín Acuña Lledó, who after 9 years of experience in the web industry decided to come together under the same ideology and work philosophy and a clear conviction that when a small group of talented people has an opportunity, it can create wonderful things.


NOS website back in 2003

Since 1997, Juanma and Martín have contributed to the growth of the Internet in Paraguay, and long before it became trendy, they were already developing applications and services that today outline the technology we use on a daily basis, like dynamic CSS websites and value-added services via SMS.


NOS 2006 website was featured on several CSS galleries

Clients NOS worked with include Fundación Moisés Bertoni, Mariscal López Shopping, ConexionGroup, Netphone, CTI, VOX, Jerovia, and the United Nations Population Funds, among others.
