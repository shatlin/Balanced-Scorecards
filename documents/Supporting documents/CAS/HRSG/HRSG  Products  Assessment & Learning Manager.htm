Assessment & Learning Manager


Overview

This module gives Users the opportunity to develop a personalized learning plan based on an Assessment, and gives both the User and Supervisor the ability to monitor and assess progress against target learning and development objectives.

Benefits

  • Create Personalized Learning Plans from competency-based assessments

Features

  • Conduct Self-Assessments
  • Generate Individual Gap Analysis Reports
  • Conduct Career Planning
  • Parallel Supervisors Assessment
  • Generate Aggregate Gap Reports (Managers)
  • Create Learning Plans
  • Identify Learning Resources

1. Conduct Self-Assessments

The Self-Assessment component enables an individual to rate him/herself against each of the competencies outlined in the job profile. Users complete and submit a questionnaire, and receive an automatically generated personal report detailing their strengths and weakness by competency grouping, by competency and by individual behaviours.

Top

2. Generate Individual Gap Analysis Reports

Individual gap analysis reports identify the User’s self-rating for each behavioural indicator. These ratings are compared with both the target rating (for the specific position) and the average rating of all others who have self-assessed against the same position. Results are displayed graphically for each question, detailing the self-rating, the target rating and the average rating of other users. A similar graphic is shown to demonstrate the rolled-up ratings for each competency. The report identifies the User’s strongest and weakest competencies, and generates a link to a learning plan template that can be completed on-line.

Top

3. Conduct Career Planning

This component enables the User to assess him/herself against any job profile in the tool. He or she selects a job profile and the Learning Manager generates a questionnaire based on that chosen profile. The individual can rate him/herself against each of the competencies outlined in the job profile, using a drop-down rating scale, and will receive an automatically generated personal report detailing their results in a variety of formats.

Top

4. Parallel Supervisors Assessment

Managers can dispatch a request for the completion of a self-assessment to employees. Once the employee has completed the assessment the manager is automatically notified. The manager then completes a parallel assessment and a gap analysis report comparing the two inputs is generated. Managers and employees can use this for discussion to develop learning plans or performance reviews.

Top

5. Generate Aggregate Gap Reports (Managers)

Aggregate Gap Reports allow the Manager to generate collective reports for all Users who have conducted Self-Assessments. Individual Departments also have the ability to generate aggregate reports for Users within their own jurisdiction. Results are displayed graphically for each question, detailing the average self-ratings and the target rating. A similar graphic will also be shown to demonstrate the rolled-up ratings for each competency. A report will also identify the overall strongest and weakest competencies within the organization / Department.

Top

6. Create Learning Plans

The Learning Plan creator enables an individual to create a learning plan to address their personal learning needs. Learning Plans can be generated from the results of a self assessment or by creating free-form items to match corporate goals or specific learning requirements. The User, with or without their Supervisor, can define how he or she will enhance a competency by determining areas for development, the outcome after development, the method of review, the time frame and the estimated cost of each action item.

Top

7. Identify Learning Resources

Within the Learning Manager, the Administrator enters learning types and learning resources (e.g., books, multi-media learning tools, on-the-job activities) and attaches these resources to the competencies in the dictionary. This information is customizable to match each User’s identified ‘Areas for Improvement’. From within the learning plan users can attach resources to their plan. The complete data base of learning resources can also be viewed and searched independent of the learning plan.
