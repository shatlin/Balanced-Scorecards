Effective Interviewing Guide

Helping you make the right hiring decisions!

HRSG's Effective Interviewing Guide sets out in an easy-to-follow manner, a structured, behavioral-based approach to conducting employment interviews. This Guide makes it easy for the Hiring Manager or Interviewer to collect relevant behavioral information about a candidate’s relevant work experiences and accomplishments.

Why Behaviour-based Interviewing?

Past behavior is the best predictor of future performance. A behavioral approach provides critical information about candidates’ past performance and accomplishments. Together with a behaviour-based Reference Check, this information can help you predict a candidate’s future performance on your team.

Learn how to:

  • Conduct reliable and valid competency-based Interviews.
  • Ask Balanced Questions.
  • Collect Behavioral Examples.
  • Identify False Behavioral Examples.
  • Take into consideration, and ensure compliance, with relevant Legislation.
