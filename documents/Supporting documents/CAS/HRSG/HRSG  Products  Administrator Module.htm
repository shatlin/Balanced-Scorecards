Administrator Module

The ADMINISTRATOR MODULE is the module or platform upon which all other modules function.

A sophisticated database of all the organization’s competencies and competency profiles**, the ADMINISTRATOR MODULE sits at the heart of i-SkillSuite™.

This Module enables the Administrator(s) (i.e. the individual(s) responsible for overseeing the editing and management of the software) the ability to set the parameters for user access to the tool’s functionality, and brings Competency-based Talent Management to all employees within the organization.

How does it work?

The organization's competencies are loaded into the Administrator, proficiency levels are assigned to the competencies, and job profiles are created. Employees can view the complete list of competencies and look at all the job profiles within an organization.

These job profiles then become the basis for:

  • Creating customised LEARNING PLANS

  • Conducting valid and effective PERFORMANCE REVIEWS

  • Building job-specific, behavioural-based INTERVIEW GUIDES

  • Conducting Competency Matching for SUCCESSION PLANNING


    **If an organization does not already have their own competencies and profiles developed, the ADMINISTRATOR can be loaded with HRSG's generic competencies
