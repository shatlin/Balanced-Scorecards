i-SkillSuite™- Talent Management Software


Leverage the investment you've already made in your people!

Built around a common competency architecture, i-SkillSuite™ is dedicated to supporting your talent management processes.

Our innovative, modular software enables you to leverage the investment you've already made in your people, whether it be through the development of employees or the development of an HR information system.

HRSG’s i-SkillSuite™ enables you to bring effective Competency-based Talent Management to all employees within the organization, via job selection and assessment processes, career development and learning tools, performance management and career planning systems.

A Flexible, Modular Approach

Developed within self-contained, web-based modules, the multi-lingual i-SkillSuite™ was designed such that any one module can work as a stand-alone product or as part of an integrated solution.

All modules are fully customizeable to reflect your organizational structure, hierarchy of competencies and unique requirements, and can be fully integrated with pre-existing corporate HR applications.
