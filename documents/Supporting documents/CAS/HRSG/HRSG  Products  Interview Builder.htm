Interview Builder Module


Overview

This module enables Managers to generate high quality interview guides built around a database of pre-approved competency-based interview questions.

Benefits

  • Generate INTERVIEW QUESTIONS.
  • View and Modify BENCHMARK GUIDES.

Features

The Interview Questionnaire is generated based on a Job Profile or Statement of Work. The appropriate job profile is selected and the related list of competencies, skills or abilities is displayed and can be selected by the Interviewer. Questions and probes can be modified, added or deleted at this point.

Once the questionnaire is finalized and saved, the Interviewer can print out the questionnaire (in pdf format) for use during the interview (and future interviews).

The Interview Guide includes a rating scale and instructions on how to conduct and evaluate competency-based interviews.
